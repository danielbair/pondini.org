There's often a bit of confusion about this terminology: Time Machine vs. Time Capsule:

  1. •Time Machine is Apple software that performs backups, to an external HD, a Time Capsule, etc.; and also allows you to browse and restore from them. It comes with Leopard and Snow Leopard, but cannot run on any previous versions of OSX.

  2. •A Time Capsule is Apple hardware that combines a wireless router and hard drive.  It can be used for Time Machine backups and/or other data.

Most Time Machine questions & problems are not unique to Time Capsules.  For those, or if you don't find what you need here, try one of these:
